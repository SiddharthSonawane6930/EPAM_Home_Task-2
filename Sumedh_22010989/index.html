Al-Hilal to offer Lionel Messi $400m per year deal after Ronaldo’s Record-breaking move to Al-Nassr

Posted on 3th April, 2023

Saudi Arabia club, Al-Hilal, have wooed Argentine superstar, Lionel Messi, with a deal worth €400 million a year. This was confirmed by football transfer expert, Fabrizio Romano, on Tuesday night. According to him, the salary on the table for Messi is worth more than $438 million per annum. However, Messi is believed to be keen on continuing his career in Europe, with Barcelona waiting on Financial Fair Play to make their move.

Al Nassr vs Al Adalah Highlights: Cristiano Ronaldo scores a brace as Al Nassr thump Al Adalah 5-0

Posted on 4th April, 2023

Cristiano Ronaldo returned to club action on Tuesday night and picked off from where he left, scoring a brace as Al Nassr demolished a hapless Al Adalah 5-0.
The two goals added to Ronaldo’s previous tally of 9, bringing him to 11 goals for the season.
